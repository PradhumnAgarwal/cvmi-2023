Accommodation Available At ABV-IIITM Gwalior

The accommodation of Student/Faculty/Industry Persons attending the conference will be arranged in the Visitor Hostel (Pocket-B) of ABV-IIITM Gwalior Campus, on payment basis which will be facilitated on a first come first serve. For booking related queries you can contact at visitorhostel@iiitm.ac.in.
Additionally, accommodation facilities will also be available in the Institute Hostels (Boys and Girls).
